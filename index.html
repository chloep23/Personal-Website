A Unity 2D game with self-created Procreate graphics that provides an accessible lens into Korean culture for the 600 students at National Cathedral School with an Asian body of less than 14%. The game’s basic concept is the following: throw the four provided sticks repeatedly and the first player to make his/her way around the board, returning to the start, wins.

Dalgona Unity Game

Tech: C#, Unity, Procreate

A fresh Unity 2D game incorporating original Procreate graphics, designed to showcase Korean media, specifically inspired by Squid Games. In a given amount of time, the player must carefully trace around the selected pattern imprinted on the honeycomb sugar candy without breaking it. Players can choose which pattern they wish to attempt, though the umbrella is notorious for its difficulty, while the triangle is the easiest.
